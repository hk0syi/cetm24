Two Approaches to Data Science

Top-Down Approach

  • Starts with a problem or business question.
  • Then looks for data & facts that can help solve that problem.
  • Guided by human thinking, often clear and fast.
  • Best when the question is known and business impact is clear.

Bottom-Up Approach

  • Starts with available data.
  • Explores what problems or opportunities can be discovered from the data.
  • Can reveal hidden patterns, insights, or new business ideas.
  • Best when exploring unstructured datasets or building data-driven platforms.
