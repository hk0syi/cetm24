Handling Non-Normal Data – Two Approaches

📊 Data not normally distributed
⬇️
❓ Decide on analysis goal
⬇️
Is the goal to use mean, SD, and reliable CIs?
⬇️
✅ YES: Transform data to approximate normality
(Log, Sqrt, Reciprocal)
⬇️
🔍 Recheck normality
⬇️
📈 If normal → Use parametric tests
(t-test, ANOVA, regression)
⬇️
🔄 Back-transform results for interpretation
❌ NO: Use non-parametric tests
(Mann–Whitney, Wilcoxon, Kruskal–Wallis)
⬇️
⚠️ Works without normality
but CIs for mean may be less reliable
