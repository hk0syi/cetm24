🎯 KDD Comprehensibility Journey

Understanding Why "Understandable" Patterns Matter in Knowledge Discovery

1

🌟 The Foundation: KDD Definition

Knowledge Discovery in Databases (KDD)

"The non-trivial process of identifying valid, novel, potentially useful, and ultimately understandable patterns in data."

— Fayyad et al., 1996

Four Quality Requirements:

✅

Valid

Statistically sound and accurate

💡

Novel

Surprising, counterintuitive, unexpected

🎯

Useful

Actionable, supports decisions

🧠

Understandable

Interpretable by humans

2

🧠 Why Comprehensibility Matters

The Human Factor

Users must interpret and validate discovered patterns. Understanding leads to:

🤝

Confidence

Trust in the results

⚡

Actionability

Better decision making

📈

Usefulness

Practical application

Domain Importance Varies

🏥 Medical/Scientific Domains

Comprehensibility is CRITICAL

Doctors need to understand why a diagnosis was made

🔍 Pattern Recognition

Accuracy may matter more

Black box models acceptable if highly accurate

Comprehensible Model Types

🌳 Decision Trees
📋 IF-THEN Rules
🔗 Bayesian Networks

⚠️ Problem

Most measures of comprehensibility are simplistic (e.g., just counting model size), ignoring semantics and actual human understanding.

4

🔍 Filtering for Interest

The Challenge

Mining produces thousands of rules → Need post-processing to find interesting ones

Two Approaches

👤 User-Driven (Subjective)

  • Uses domain knowledge
  • Captures user beliefs & preferences
  • Focuses on novelty from user's perspective
  • Personalized surprisingness

📊 Data-Driven (Objective)

  • Statistical measures (support, confidence, lift)
  • Generic algorithms
  • User-independent
  • Standardized metrics

🎯 Goal

Balance objective validity with subjective novelty and usefulness

5

🎭 Practical Solution: User Templates (User-driven approach)

How It Works

A rule is interesting if it matches an inclusive template but NOT a restrictive template.

✅ Inclusive Templates

Specify what IS interesting

"Show me rules involving medical side effects"
"Find patterns in customer behavior during holidays"

❌ Restrictive Templates

Exclude trivial/uninteresting rules

"Exclude rules that are already known medical facts"
"Filter out demographic obviousness"

🏆 Result

Discovered knowledge that matches novelty, usefulness, and understandability criteria for your specific domain!

Key Takeaways

🧠 Comprehensibility is essential for user trust and action
⚠️ But accuracy + comprehensibility ≠ interesting
🔍 Post-processing selection is crucial
⚖️ Combine objective and subjective filtering methods
🎯 User templates help ensure domain-relevant novelty

🌟 Final Insight

Comprehensibility is the bridge between data mining algorithms and human decision-making. Without it, even the most accurate patterns remain unused in practice.
