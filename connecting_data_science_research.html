Connecting Research and Data Science

Careful, Patient Study ‚Üí Problem Definition
Start with curiosity and observation
Research begins with curiosity; data science begins by clearly defining the problem.
A clear question ensures measurable outcomes and focused investigation.
Systematic Investigation ‚Üí Data Collection
Organize your study with a clear purpose and structure
Research gathers data methodically; data science collects structured data from various sources.
Systematic collection ensures relevance and reliability.
Establish Facts & Principles ‚Üí Data Cleaning
Analyze evidence to uncover patterns and truths
Researchers validate facts; data scientists clean and preprocess data to ensure accuracy.
Clean data leads to reliable models and insights.
Structured Inquiry ‚Üí Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA)
Ask focused questions and testable hypotheses
Exploration helps shape hypotheses in both fields through visualizations and summaries.
Understanding data patterns leads to better modeling and conclusions.
Scientific Methodology ‚Üí Data Modeling
Use experiments, data collection, and analysis
Researchers create experiments; data scientists build models to simulate outcomes.
Modeling enables prediction and hypothesis testing at scale.
Solve Problems ‚Üí Deployment
Apply results to find answers and improve understanding
Research findings inform decisions; data models are deployed into systems.
Solutions must be used in the real world to drive value.
Create New Knowledge ‚Üí Reporting
Contribute findings to the broader community
Research is published; data science results are reported and visualized.
Knowledge grows when shared and understood by others.
General Applicability ‚Üí Monitoring & Maintenance
Make sure your findings apply in other situations
Researchers test generalizability; data scientists monitor models post-deployment.
Continuous validation ensures relevance over time.
